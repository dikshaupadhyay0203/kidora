Kidora is an interactive learning platform for kids, designed to make education fun and engaging through stories, rhymes, quizzes, and more!

You can change the theme by clicking on the "Theme" button in the top-right corner and selecting your favorite one from the dropdown menu.

Visit the "Progress Level" section from the sidebar to see your performance in different subjects and quizzes.

You earn coins by completing quizzes and other activities. These coins can be used to unlock new features and content in the future!

The stories and rhymes are generated by an advanced AI model. You can give it a topic, and it will create a unique story or rhyme for you!
