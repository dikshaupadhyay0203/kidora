🌟 Welcome to Kidora!

Kidora is your magical jungle buddy! 🐾
A fun learning app where kids explore maths, science, stories & more — all through rhymes, games, and rewards! 🎉

🧠 How It Works

🦜 Ask a question or pick a topic.
🦁 Get answers in fun rhymes or mini stories.
🎵 Enjoy music, voice, and animations.
🍌 Earn coins and bananas for daily learning!
